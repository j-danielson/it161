Audience Information
Audience Issues Approach
"Hoagie" Customers

Ages 40 - 80 with basic computer skills. Mostly accessing site on mobile phones using safari or chrome. If on desktop, will most likely use defualt browsers like Edge. Have been shopping here since it was "Hoagie's Corner" in the 70's.
  • Looking for deli menu and hours
  • Wanting conveince of looking online
  • wants to order online in advance
  • Include link to menu at top and first option to make it easier to find
  • Upload image of the original menu sign that's been used since the 70's. Have text menu underneath for accessibility and limited brandwidth users.
  • Have simple ordering system for customers to add sandwiches to cart and allow alteration to condiments, bread, and meat.
"Beer" Customers

Ages 20-40 and computer savvy. Typical browsers will be chrome and safari on mobile. Enjoys trying local breweries and always trying the latest beer trend.
  • Looking for a Tap list and what new beers we are carrying this week
  • Wants to know store hours and when new beer comes in stock
  • Wants a convient way to order a keg through the store
  • Have a page with current tap list that includes beer and brewery information
  • Include a sign up form to receive a weekly newsletter that lists new beer in stock and new breweries they started to carry
  • Have a contact form for customers to fill out to get basic information for kegs they want to order and provide basic details on how ordering works and the fees associated with it.
Breweries

Typically marketing manager or company representative. Is computer savvy and familiar with reaching out to companies and locating information. Most likely to use Chrome or Sarafi browser on desktop.
  • Looking for companies that can carry their beer or to make a collabration beer with
  • Looking for owner contact information
  • Checking reviews, policies, or morals to see if it will provide a good representation for the company to work together
  • Include pictures of the store working together with other breweries
  • Have contact information at the bottom of the page to send an email to the owners
  • Inlcude a landing page on the website to highlight accomplishments, fundraising events, and donations that the company has worked with in the past
Local Companies

Ages range from late 20s to 40 and is typically women. Has basic computer skills and usually using Safari browser on desktop
  • Looking for contact information to reach out to the owner
  • Checking to see if we stock products from small local companies
  • Looking for more information on how we split profits or how much product we buy
  • Have contact information at the bottom of the page
  • have a small paragraph at the bottom of the home page letting them know to reach out if they're interested in selling with us and if they have any questions
